Enter Play Mode.

Play Mode is used during the physical tabletop match. Open the full-screen tracker to manage your active Warband, Command Points, Shard bonuses, unit HP, temporary stat changes, activations, actions, and destroyed units.

Live Match Tools

What the tracker handles

Command Points

Track base CP, Shard bonus CP, current CP, and CP spent to activate units during the turn.

Match Resource

Health Points

Track HP below or above base values, such as 1/5 after damage or 10/5 after an ability, counter, or temporary buff.

Live Unit State

Stat Modifiers

Adjust MR, AP, AR, and DR during play to reflect active effects, debuffs, enhancements, counters, and abilities.

Temporary Effects

Activations

Activate a unit once per turn, spend CP, then choose up to two actions from Move, Attack, Claim, and Ability.

Turn Control

Destroyed Units

Mark a unit as destroyed to grey it out while keeping its information visible for reference.

Battlefield State

Warband Attributes

View faction, role, Influence, Coalition Key, Veto Flag, and Warlord Command Points while playing.

Reference Data
