Round Flow

Turn structure draft shell

1. Start of Round

Resolve start-of-round effects, refresh temporary resources, and prepare units for activation.

2. Unit Activations

Players alternate activating units, spending actions, moving, attacking, using abilities, and playing tactical cards.

3. End of Round

Resolve lasting effects, check objectives, remove expired statuses, score points, and prepare for the next round.

Unit Language

Stat glossary draft

HP

Health Points. Tracks how much damage a unit can take before being removed or defeated.

AP

Action Points. Determines how many actions a unit can spend during its activation.

IP

Ixon Points. The army-building cost used to construct balanced warbands.

Role

Defines battlefield purpose, such as commander, infantry, ranged, heavy, support, or specialist.

Faction

Identifies the force, culture, empire, guild, order, or origin a unit belongs to.

Traits

Special keywords that alter how a unit moves, fights, reacts, or interacts with rules.
